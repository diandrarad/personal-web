about me

I'm a final-year computer science student on the thrilling yet perplexing journey of figuring it all out. Passionate about delving into the realms of data science, I find solace in coding amidst the complexities of algorithms and data sets.

Beyond the screens, I'm navigating the enchanting world of literature, despite the peculiar dance my mind engages in. Minimalism resonates with me, both in design and ideology, as I strive to untangle the clutter of thoughts and code.

Amidst the pixels and lines of code, I carry the nuances of being neurodivergent, embracing the unique perspectives that come with it. My website is a canvas where curiosity and creativity converge, a space where the intersectionality of my identity finds its silent expression. Join me on this expedition, where technology meets introspection, and coding is an art form.
